### phi

phi = (root(5) + 1) / 2
phi = fib(n) / fib(n-1)

phi + 1 = phi * phi
phi - 1 = 1 / phi
phi^3 = (2 * phi) + 1
phi^2 = (root(5) + 3) / 2
